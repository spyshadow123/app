KARUNADHAM TEMPLE

ABOUT

Beautiful white marble temple of Goddess Mahalaxmi, Lord Hanuman & Lord Ganesh.There is a large prayer hall at the basement. Campus has a small garden, seats to sit and a separate underground room near the entrance to keep footwear. Photography of Mahalaxmi idol is strictly prohibited. The temple is illuminated with colorful lights on festivals especially during DiwaliThe peaceful place you will find around in nehru nagar is here , the deep silence serenity with the chanting words and the echoing sound of bells can sooth the soul of ones . Best place to came along with family or individuals.There are different 5 temples inside the campus and an aashram too.A good place in Bhopal to find peace of mind and feel your soul. Temple is surrounded by cherish environment and here parking for bikes is available.

ABOUT

Big park with many arts model made of iron and waste material. Water facility is available. People come for studies, for picnic, gardens to sit and enjoy the nature peacefully, for morning and evening walk, for exercise. Fall between MP Nagar and new market. You will often see couples romancing and make moments. Good place for gatherings and meet. Good place for young children there is slider, grounds.This place shows the lost glory of once highly popular parks of bhopal. The remains of those are still very evident and it does tells the history of lost empire. However people still find this place interesting and we can see visitors throughout the day. In Chinar Park, we got to see many articles made of iron. Many animal statues were made here using iron waste material. Here you will get to see the statue of crocodile.

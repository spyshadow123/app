PARKS

There are 35 parks found in Bhopal, MP. This is more than half of all the tourist attractions listed in this city. You may find a list of all categories of Bhopal attractions on this page to choose from.Following some name of the parks found in Bhopal.

Chinar Park

Chinar Park is one of the main parks of Bhopal city. Chinar Park is the biggest park of Bhopal city. In this you will find many artifacts to see. These artifacts are made from iron and waste materials. You will find greenery all around in the park. Inside Chinar Park you also get to see the temple of Ganesh ji and Durga ji. This temple is found in front of the main gate in Chinar Park. This park is spread over a very large area. There are many swings in the park, which attract a lot of children. You can come to this park for a walk with the family. A great time can be spent here. Chinar Park is situated in the heart of Bhopal city. Chinar Park is very beautiful.

One gets to see greenery all around in Chinar Park. Beautiful flowering plants are also found here. Different types of plants are found here. Here you can spend a good time sitting in the green grass in the garden. Morning walkers and evening walkers come and roam here in Chinar Park. There is a huge walkway here, where one can roam comfortably and enjoy the greenery of the park.

Ekant Park

Ekant Park is one od the famous garden of Bhopal With its lush forested areas and water streams.It is situated near the Shahpura Lake.It has so much flora and fauna.It has so many medicinal plants and a small bird sanctuary within the park.The best time to visit is in the early hours of the day or later in the evening.The park remains closed on Mondays.Bhopal is a city of parks with more than 32 parks spread thru out the city. But the best and must visit park is the City forest or Ekant Park. Best visited early morning it is a nature lover's delight. Well maintained with nice walking trails, you will enjoy every bit of your walk.another jewelin the crown of city of parks lakes .ekant is the best well maintained grassy lawns lush green whiff of freshair ad chipping of birds really heaven for nature lovers. Located next to the Lake, the place is great for a jog or morning walks. The sublocality Ekant Park, Char Imli falls in Bhopal district situated in Madhya Pradesh state, with a population 4510. The male and female populations are 2348 and 2162 respectively. The size of the area is about 0.77 square kilometer.

Kamla Park

Bhopal, the capital of Madhya Pradesh is a fascinating place for all the curious nature lovers. The greenest city of India is bordered by two famous lakes namely Upper lake and Lower lake.The historical city has always registered its name in the annals of history. Today you can find the imprints of its glorious past scattered throughout the nook and corners of the city. Busy yet beautiful Bhopal is known for its scenic beauty, historical places and modern infrastructure. The city is adorned with numerous parks and gardens. One amongst them is famous Kamla Park . One should take out time to visit this beautiful park in Bhopal. Amongst many other parks in Bhopal Kamla Park has special significance as being one of the most visited and beautiful park in the 'City of Lakes' Bhopal.

Kamla Park is located near the famous Upper Lake . One should never miss visiting this park while traveling to Bhopal. The park is a favorite place among teenagers and love birds. It is a famous picnic spot for families in Bhopal. At the entry of the park there is a big imposing gate having two architectural domes . The park is surrounded by the countless trees and flowers and its greenery is instrumental in attracting tourists from far and wide. The park is located very near to the famous Upper Lake in Bhopal, which itself is a famous picnic spot in Bhopal. Known for its exquisite beauty and aloofness, Upper lake Bhopal is one of the finest picnic spot in the city. Here you can experience boating in various types of boats.

Van Vihar National Park

Van Vihar National Park and Zoo is one of the most revered National Park and Zoo of Central India and is a shining beacon in the field of conservation. It is an amazing world of winged creatures, free ranging ungulates and captive carnivores in the midst of greenery.Van Vihar National Park and Zoo is one of the most revered National Parks and Zoo in Central India. It has become a shining beacon in the field of conservation and is an ideal example of eco-restoration. It is a national park situated within the city limits of Bhopal and presents a perfect picture of grandeur and tranquillity of a forest area. It is, in true sense, a real treasure trove of nature and wildlife to be explored by both the wildlife enthusiasts as well as a casual tourist.

Van Vihar National Park and Zoo is one of the most revered National Park and Zoo of Central India and is a shining beacon in the field of conservation. It is an amazing world of winged creatures, free ranging ungulates and captive carnivores in the midst of greenery. The park which is more popularly known as just “Van Vihar” is considered as a “must see” destination for tourists visiting Bhopal. The characteristic combination of rich wetland areas, meadows, pools of water at different places, rugged slopes, mixed bamboo vegetation along with grass covered plateau and areas of mixed plantations support the rich floral and faunal diversity in the area. This green oasis amidst the city of lakes also plays a very vital role as a carbon sink contributing to environmental health of Bhopal. It is situated right next to the famous Upper Lake of Bhopal also known as the “Bada Talab” which is a Ramsar Site and one of the two lakes of Bhoj Wetland.
